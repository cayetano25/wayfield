# Wayfield Pricing and Feature Gating

## Free — $0
- 1 organization
- 1 organizer
- up to 2 active workshops
- up to 75 participants per workshop
- scheduling
- public workshop page
- logistics and hotel info
- leader invitations
- leader profile completion
- participant join by code
- session selection
- self-check-in
- leader roster access for assigned sessions
- basic organizer notifications
- core offline access
- basic email verification and transactional emails

## Starter — $49/month
Everything in Free, plus:
- up to 5 organization managers
- up to 10 active workshops
- up to 250 participants per workshop
- optional session/event capacity enforcement
- waitlists
- leader day-of-session notifications
- branded pages
- light custom domain support
- reminder automation
- basic analytics
- attendance summaries

## Pro — $129/month
Everything in Starter, plus:
- unlimited or high-volume workshops
- advanced automation
- segmentation
- multi-workshop reporting
- API access
- webhooks
- advanced role-based permissions
- priority support

## Enterprise — Custom
- SSO
- MFA-ready enterprise auth support
- advanced governance
- white-label
- SLA
- onboarding
- custom integrations

## Leader messaging note
Leader day-of-session notifications should be treated as a Starter-tier feature or higher and must always remain constrained by:
- assigned session participant scope
- time-window enforcement
